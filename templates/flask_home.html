Welcome to ANN Machine Learning Visualisation Website

About this page

       This page is to simulate live updated electricity consumption and ANN forecasting model. The main objective of this website is to draw users' attention towards energy usage. Navigation links can direct you to each appliances dashborad page. Each page consists of three graphs. One is the lived updated graph with ANN forecasting model. The second figure is a sunburst pie chart, which is an interactive chart. The chart layers are divided by months, by weeks, by days, and by hour accordingly. By clicking on the centre,the chart would display the proportion of consumption based on the timeframe with the tooltip popping up, simultaneously. The third one is a bar chart. The function of the bar chart is not only to summarise how much electricity was consumed in one month, but also to reveal every half-hour consumption data piling up from the bottom to the top of the bar

Material

       The data was recorded by sensors that connecting to the appliances deployed in an open plan office located in London. Each data entry is in half hour, starting from 23rd of December in 2019 to 7th of June in 2020.
